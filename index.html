Getting here — Make a day of it

Coming by train

Trains go regularly between Charing Cross (1hr 45mins) or London Bridge (1hr 35mins) and Hastings Station. Then it’s a 25 minute walk or 5-10 minute taxi ride.

Coming by car

For those of you who plan to drive, there is no parking at the venue, but the venue has complimentary visitor parking vouchers for street parking in the immediate area and there are numerous car parks within walking distance.
