Camilla and Tom

We are getting married in a lovely walled garden, followed by champagne and a BBQ. We want everyone to relax and enjoy our special occasion, so here’s some information that might be useful.

Getting there — Make a day of it

Coming by train

Trains go regularly between Charing Cross (1hr 45mins) or London Bridge (1hr 35mins) and Hastings Station. Then it’s a 25 minute walk or 5-10 minute taxi ride.

Coming by car

For those of you who plan to drive, there's no parking at the venue. There are a few pay-and-display car parks within walking distance, and free road parking if you get lucky, but high summer at the seaside will be busy! We suggest taking taxis or public transport where possible.

What to wear

It’s a summer garden wedding by the sea, so have fun dressing up but don’t feel like you have to wear a tie or a fancy hat. Ladies may want to avoid stilettos on the grass. Shawls and jackets might be needed later on in the evening.

In the unlikely event of extreme weather, the ceremony and celebrations will be hosted indoors at the venue.
